> Our Mission

A community of hackers, engineers, and educators who are dedicated to learning about security, privacy, and user-freedom. We learn async on Discord, publish papers, push code, hack & tinker.

Hackers think. They analyze. They tinker, take-apart, modify, question, experiment, and re-build. Fueled by curiosity, they understand that at bottom, they are the ones in control. That if their computer, their program, or any other device does not work, it is the fault of the user and is an opportunity for learning. They understand that actions speak louder than words. They judge others by ideas, actions, and merit; not by skin color, nationality, or religious bias. Every hacker brings a unique perspective, skill set, background, and experience. Differences in skills, thoughts, values, and ideas allow us to exchange information and do what we do best: hack.

Come curious, stay humble, keep hacking.
